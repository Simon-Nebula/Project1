ABOUT

Copic (コピック, Kopikku) is a Japanese brand of markers made and created by Too Corporations. The markers were created in 1987 (35 years ago) and have been dominating the artistic alcohol marker market ever since its debut.


The markers come in 358 different colors and shades, making it, not only the most popular marker, but also the marker with the biggest color range. Additionally, copics also offer the ability of blendable colors, unlocking even more colors than the original 358
